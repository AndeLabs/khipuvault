## 1. Executive Summary

KhipuVault is the first Bitcoin-native savings protocol built on Mezo, offering non-custodial savings pools with automated yield generation through integration with Mezo's Stability Pool.

We won 1st place in the Mezo Hackathon (Financial Access Track) and have since deployed a complete production-ready platform on testnet.

> **We're not asking for funding to start. We're asking for support to launch safely and scale.**
