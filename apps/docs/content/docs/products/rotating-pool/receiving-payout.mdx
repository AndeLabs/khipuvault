## Understanding Your Payout

### Base Payout Calculation

```
Members: 10
Contribution per member: 100 MUSD
Base pot: 10 √ó 100 = 1,000 MUSD
```

### Enhanced with Yields

<Callout type="success">
**Bonus: Contributions earn yields while held in the pool.**

The longer funds sit before distribution, the more yields accumulate.
</Callout>

**Example calculation:**

```
Base pot: 1,000 MUSD
Holding period: 7 days average
Yield rate: 12% APY ‚Üí ~0.23% per week
Yields earned: 1,000 √ó 0.23% = 2.30 MUSD

Yields after platform fee (10%):
2.30 √ó 0.90 = 2.07 MUSD

Final payout: 1,000 + 2.07 = 1,002.07 MUSD
```

**Factors affecting yield amount:**

1. **Position in rotation**
   - Early recipients (Round 1-3): Lower yields (less time)
   - Middle recipients (Round 4-7): Moderate yields
   - Late recipients (Round 8-10): Higher yields (more time)

2. **Market conditions**
   - APY fluctuates: 10-18% typically
   - Bull markets: Higher yields
   - Bear markets: Lower yields

3. **Pool size**
   - Larger pots ‚Üí More absolute yield
   - 10 members √ó $100 = $2-3 yields
   - 20 members √ó $100 = $4-6 yields

<Callout type="info">
**Why collateral is returned:**

You deposited collateral to protect the pool in case you received early and then defaulted. Once you receive your payout, you still owe future contributions, so the collateral is returned as you no longer pose default risk (you haven't been paid yet relative to others).
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
**Don't default after receiving!**

This is the most critical time. Some members are tempted to default after getting their payout. Don't be that person. Your collateral was already returned, and defaulting now will:

- Trigger penalties
- Damage your reputation permanently
- Get you banned from future pools
- Hurt your fellow members
</Callout>

## Handling Issues

### Payout Delay

**Problem:** Round date passed but you haven't received payout.

**Causes:**
1. Some members haven't paid yet
2. Technical issue with distribution
3. Organizer manually reviewing

**Solution:**
```
1. Check pool dashboard: See payment status
2. Contact organizer: Ask for update
3. View contract: Check if funds are ready
4. Manual claim: Try claiming if available
```

### Incorrect Amount

**Problem:** Received different amount than expected.

**Causes:**
1. Late payment penalties applied
2. Market yield changes
3. Missing contributions from members

**Solution:**
```
1. Review breakdown: Check payout details
2. Verify on-chain: Compare with smart contract
3. Contact organizer: If discrepancy found
4. Dispute if needed: On-chain evidence
```

### Wallet Issues

**Problem:** Can't access wallet to receive payout.

**Prevention:**
```
Before your round:
- Backup seed phrase securely
- Test wallet access
- Ensure wallet app is updated
- Have recovery plan
```

**Emergency solution:**
```
If locked out:
1. Recover wallet with seed phrase
2. Contact organizer for delay (if manual claim)
3. Use recovery service if needed
```

## Special Scenarios

### Early Payout (Round 1-2)

**Pros:**
- Get lump sum immediately
- Use for urgent needs
- Start benefiting early

**Cons:**
- Lower yields (less time to accumulate)
- Longer commitment after receiving
- Higher default temptation

**Example:**
```
Round 1 payout: 1,000.50 MUSD (minimal yields)
Remaining payments: 9 √ó 100 = 900 MUSD
Duration: 9 more months
```

### Late Payout (Round 8-10)

**Pros:**
- Higher yields (more time accumulating)
- Shorter commitment after receiving
- Most members already proven reliable

**Cons:**
- Wait longer for lump sum
- Paid more before receiving
- Slight default risk from early recipients

**Example:**
```
Round 10 payout: 1,015 MUSD (max yields)
Remaining payments: 0 (COMPLETE!)
Duration: Done immediately after
```
