## Prize Claim Troubleshooting

### "Prize Not Found"

**Cause**: You didn't win, or wrong week number

**Solution**:
```
1. Double-check winner announcement
2. Verify your address matches
3. Check correct week number
4. Confirm draw has occurred
```

### "Already Claimed"

**Cause**: Prize was already claimed (possibly by you)

**Solution**:
```
1. Check your transaction history
2. Verify MUSD balance increased
3. Contact support if balance unchanged
```

### "Claim Expired"

**Cause**: Tried to claim after 90-day deadline

**Solution**:
```
Unfortunately, expired prizes cannot be recovered.
They're returned to the reserve to fund future prizes.

Prevention: Enable email notifications
```

### "Insufficient Gas"

**Cause**: Not enough MBTC for gas fees

**Solution**:
```
1. Acquire ~0.001 MBTC from Mezo faucet or exchange
2. Send to your wallet
3. Retry claim transaction
```
