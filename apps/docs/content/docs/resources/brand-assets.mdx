### Logo Spacing

**Minimum clear space:** Equal to the height of the "K" in KhipuVault

<Callout type="info">
Always maintain clear space around the logo. Don't place text, graphics, or other logos within the clear space.
</Callout>

### Minimum Sizes

**Digital:**
- Minimum width: 120px
- Recommended: 180px+

**Print:**
- Minimum width: 1 inch (2.54cm)
- Recommended: 1.5 inches+

### Logo Don'ts

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Change logo colors (except approved variations)
- Rotate or distort
- Add effects (drop shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place on busy backgrounds (use solid colors)
- Stretch or squash
- Recreate or redraw
- Use old versions

## Boilerplate Text

### Short Description (50 words)

> KhipuVault is a decentralized Bitcoin savings platform built on Mezo blockchain. Earn yields through Individual Savings, Community Pools, Rotating Pools (ROSCAs), or Prize Pools. Non-custodial, transparent, and inspired by Andean savings traditions. Your keys, your Bitcoin.

### Long Description (150 words)

> KhipuVault is a decentralized Bitcoin savings platform that brings traditional Andean savings wisdom to the blockchain. Built on Mezo, a Bitcoin layer 2, KhipuVault offers four innovative products: Individual Savings for personal yield generation, Community Pools for group savings with higher returns, Rotating Pools that digitize traditional ROSCAs/pasanakus, and Prize Pools offering no-loss lottery experiences.
>
> The platform is completely non-custodial - users maintain full control of their Bitcoin at all times. All smart contracts are open-source, audited, and built with OpenZeppelin security standards. KhipuVault combines the security of Bitcoin with the flexibility of smart contracts to create a transparent, accessible savings platform for everyone.
>
> Founded in 2025, KhipuVault is currently in testnet phase on Mezo, with mainnet launch planned for Q2 2026.

### Quick Facts

- **Founded:** 2025
- **Platform:** Mezo blockchain (Bitcoin L2)
- **Status:** Testnet (mainnet Q2 2026)
- **Users:** 2,300+ (testnet)
- **TVL:** $2.5M (testnet)
- **Team Size:** 8
- **Open Source:** Yes
- **Audited:** Yes (Slither, Aderyn)
