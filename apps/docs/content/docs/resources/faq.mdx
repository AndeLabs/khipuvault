## Getting Started

### What is KhipuVault?

KhipuVault is a **decentralized Bitcoin savings platform** built on the Mezo blockchain. It offers four innovative products:

1. **Individual Savings** - Personal savings with yields
2. **Community Pools** - Group savings with higher yields
3. **Rotating Pool (ROSCA)** - Traditional rotating savings digitized
4. **Prize Pool** - No-loss lottery with Bitcoin

All funds remain in your control (non-custodial) and can be withdrawn anytime.

### Do I need crypto experience to use KhipuVault?

No! Our platform is designed for beginners. You'll need to:

- Install MetaMask wallet (5 minutes)
- Add Mezo network (1 click)
- Get test MUSD from faucet (free)
- Make your first deposit (2 clicks)

Follow our [Getting Started Guide](/docs/getting-started) for step-by-step instructions.

### Is KhipuVault free to use?

Yes, the platform is free. You only pay:

- **Gas fees** for blockchain transactions (usually < $0.50)

No monthly fees, no hidden charges, no deposit/withdrawal fees.

### What is a Community Pool?

A **Community Pool** is shared savings where members pool funds together to earn higher yields through collective deposits.

**How it works:**
1. Create a pool or join existing one
2. Members deposit Bitcoin (MUSD)
3. Pool earns yields together
4. Each member keeps their share
5. Withdraw anytime (your portion only)

**Benefits:**
- Higher yields than individual savings
- Save with friends/family/community
- Transparent on blockchain
- Still maintain custody

[Learn more](/docs/products/community-pool)

### What is a Rotating Pool (ROSCA)?

A **Rotating Pool** is a digital version of traditional "tandas", "pasanakus", or ROSCAs (Rotating Savings and Credit Associations).

**How it works:**
1. 5-10 people commit to equal monthly deposits
2. Each cycle, one member receives the full pot
3. Rotates until everyone receives their turn
4. Enforced by smart contracts

**Example:**
- 10 members √ó $100/month = $1,000 pot
- Month 1: Alice receives $1,000
- Month 2: Bob receives $1,000
- ... continues for 10 months

[Learn more](/docs/products/rotating-pool)

### What is the Prize Pool?

A **Prize Pool** is a no-loss lottery. Deposit Bitcoin, earn yields, and win prizes - without risking your principal.

**How it works:**
1. Deposit MUSD into prize pool
2. Pool generates yields collectively
3. Weekly/monthly prize drawings
4. Winners receive accumulated yields
5. Withdraw principal anytime

**Benefits:**
- Can't lose your deposit
- Chance to win big prizes
- Fun way to save
- Withdraw anytime

[Learn more](/docs/products/prize-pool)

## Yields & Earnings

### How do yields work?

Yields come from **deploying pooled Bitcoin** into various DeFi strategies:

- Mezo staking rewards
- Bitcoin lending protocols
- Liquidity provision
- MEZ token incentives (future)

Your deposit earns yields automatically. Earnings are distributed weekly.

### What are the typical yields?

**Current yields (testnet):**
- Individual Savings: ~5-8% APY
- Community Pools: ~8-12% APY
- Rotating Pools: Varies (lump sum)
- Prize Pools: Prizes from collective yields

‚ö†Ô∏è **Yields are variable** and depend on market conditions.

### How are yields calculated?

**Formula:**
```
Your Yield = (Your Deposit / Total Pool) √ó Pool Earnings
```

**Example:**
- You deposit: 1,000 MUSD
- Total pool: 100,000 MUSD (you're 1%)
- Pool earns: 500 MUSD this week
- Your share: 500 √ó 1% = 5 MUSD

### When are yields distributed?

**Distribution Schedule:**

| Product | Frequency | Method |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Individual Savings | Weekly | Auto-compound |
| Community Pool | Weekly | Auto-compound |
| Rotating Pool | End of cycle | Lump sum |
| Prize Pool | Weekly drawing | Prize to winners |

### Can I compound my yields?

**Yes, automatically!**

Yields are added to your principal and earn yields too.

**Example:**
- Week 1: Deposit 1,000 MUSD, earn 10 MUSD
- Week 2: Now earning on 1,010 MUSD
- Week 3: Now earning on 1,020.10 MUSD
- Compounding effect grows over time

### Are yields guaranteed?

**No.** Yields are **variable** and depend on:

- DeFi protocol performance
- Market conditions
- Total pool deposits
- Network activity

<Callout type="warning">
Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Yields can go up or down.
</Callout>

### What's the difference between APY and APR?

**APR (Annual Percentage Rate)**
- Simple interest (no compounding)
- Example: 10% APR = 10% per year

**APY (Annual Percentage Yield)**
- Compound interest (reinvesting earnings)
- Example: 10% APY = ~10.52% effective rate

KhipuVault displays APY because we auto-compound yields.

### Can yields be negative?

In theory yes, but unlikely:

- Smart contracts could lose funds (rare)
- DeFi protocols could fail
- We use conservative strategies to minimize risk

If yields are negative, we **pause operations** until resolved.

## Technical Issues

### Why is my transaction failing?

**Common causes:**

1. **Insufficient gas**
   - Solution: Increase gas limit by 20%

2. **Insufficient MUSD balance**
   - Solution: Check balance, reduce amount

3. **Not approved MUSD spending**
   - Solution: Click "Approve" before "Deposit"

4. **Slippage too low** (doesn't apply to KhipuVault)

5. **Contract paused**
   - Solution: Check [status page](https://status.khipuvault.com)

[Full troubleshooting guide](/docs/resources/troubleshooting)

### My transaction is pending forever. What do I do?

**Solutions:**

**Option 1: Speed Up**
1. Click pending transaction in MetaMask
2. Click "Speed Up"
3. Increase gas price by 10-20%
4. Confirm

**Option 2: Cancel**
1. Click pending transaction
2. Click "Cancel"
3. Submit cancellation (must pay gas)

**Option 3: Wait**
- Transactions usually confirm within 5 minutes
- If network is congested, may take longer

### Why can't I connect my wallet?

**Troubleshooting:**

1. **Refresh the page**
2. **Check MetaMask is unlocked**
3. **Switch to Mezo Testnet**
4. **Update MetaMask** to latest version
5. **Try different browser** (Chrome recommended)
6. **Disable other wallet extensions** (conflicts)
7. **Clear cache and cookies**

Still stuck? Ask in [Discord #support](https://discord.gg/khipuvault)

### The website is slow or not loading

**Quick fixes:**

1. **Refresh the page** (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
2. **Clear browser cache**
3. **Try different browser**
4. **Check your internet connection**
5. **Try mobile data** (to rule out WiFi issues)
6. **Check status page**: [status.khipuvault.com](https://status.khipuvault.com)

If down for everyone, we're working on it!

### I got an "Out of Gas" error

**Cause:** Transaction ran out of gas before completing.

**Solution:**
1. Click "Edit" on gas settings in MetaMask
2. Increase "Gas Limit" by 20-30%
3. Keep "Gas Price" the same (unless you want faster)
4. Retry transaction

**Prevention:** Use "Fast" gas setting for complex transactions.

### My balance is showing incorrectly

**Troubleshooting:**

1. **Refresh the page** (data might be cached)
2. **Check on block explorer** (source of truth)
   - Go to [explorer.test.mezo.org](https://explorer.test.mezo.org)
   - Search your wallet address
   - Verify actual balance
3. **Clear browser cache**
4. **Disconnect and reconnect wallet**

If explorer shows correct but UI doesn't: Wait 5 mins or contact support.
