### Quick Answers

<Callout type="info" title="Can I lose my Bitcoin?">
KhipuVault is non-custodial - you always control your funds. However, standard crypto risks apply: smart contract bugs, lost keys, user error. See [Security](/docs/security) for details.
</Callout>

<Callout type="success" title="How do I withdraw?">
Go to your pool, click "Withdraw", enter amount, and confirm. Funds arrive in your wallet within seconds. No lock-up periods (except ROSCA commitments).
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="What are the fees?">
- Deposits: Free (pay gas only)
- Withdrawals: Free (pay gas only)
- Yield: 10% performance fee (deducted from earnings)
</Callout>
