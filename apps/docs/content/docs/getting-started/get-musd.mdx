# Get MUSD Test Tokens

To try KhipuVault on testnet, you need **MUSD** (Mezo USD) - a Bitcoin-pegged stablecoin for testing.

<Callout type="success" title="Completely Free">
Testnet tokens are **100% free** and have **no real-world value**. They're perfect for learning and testing KhipuVault without any financial risk!
</Callout>

**MUSD** (Mezo USD) is a stablecoin on the Mezo blockchain:

## Get MUSD from Faucet

A "faucet" is a service that gives free testnet tokens. Mezo provides an official faucet:

<div className="fd-steps [[[<Steps>_h3]_h3]_h3]:fd-step">

### Visit the Mezo Faucet

Go to: [https://faucet.test.mezo.org](https://faucet.test.mezo.org)

### Connect Your Wallet

1. Click "Connect Wallet"
2. Select MetaMask
3. Approve the connection

### Verify Network

Make sure your MetaMask is on **Mezo Testnet**. If not:
1. Open MetaMask
2. Switch to "Mezo Testnet"
3. Refresh the faucet page

### Request Tokens

1. Click "Get MUSD" or "Request Tokens"
2. Wait 10-30 seconds for the transaction to complete
3. Check your MetaMask - you should see MUSD appear!

### Verify Balance

Open MetaMask and verify you have MUSD. You should see:
- **MUSD** in your token list
- Amount: Usually 1,000 MUSD or similar

</div>

<Callout type="info">
**Faucet Limits**: To prevent abuse, faucets usually limit how often you can request tokens (e.g., once per 24 hours per address).
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

### MUSD Not Showing Up

1. **Wait**: Transactions can take 10-30 seconds
2. **Refresh MetaMask**: Close and reopen the extension
3. **Import manually**: Follow the "Add MUSD to MetaMask" section above
4. **Check explorer**: Visit [explorer.test.mezo.org](https://explorer.test.mezo.org) and search your address

### Faucet Says "Rate Limited"

- You've already requested tokens recently
- Wait 24 hours and try again
- OR ask for tokens in Discord

### Wrong Network

If you see this error:
1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown
3. Select "Mezo Testnet"
4. Try the faucet again

### "Insufficient Funds" Error

You need a tiny amount of BTC for gas fees. The faucet should give both MUSD and BTC. If you only have MUSD:
1. Try the faucet again (it might give BTC)
2. Ask in Discord for a small BTC transfer
