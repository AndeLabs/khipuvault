# What is KhipuVault?

KhipuVault is a **decentralized savings platform** that brings Bitcoin to everyone through innovative, community-driven products inspired by Andean savings traditions.

## The Vision

Traditional banking excludes billions of people worldwide. KhipuVault democratizes access to Bitcoin-based savings by combining:

- üèîÔ∏è **Andean wisdom** - Centuries-old community savings traditions (ROSCAs/Pasanakus)
- ‚õìÔ∏è **Blockchain technology** - Transparent, trustless, and secure
- ‚Çø **Bitcoin foundation** - Built on the most secure cryptocurrency

## How It Works

KhipuVault operates on the **Mezo blockchain**, a Bitcoin Layer-2 that enables smart contracts while maintaining Bitcoin's security guarantees.

### The Platform

```
User's Bitcoin ‚Üí Mezo Blockchain ‚Üí Smart Contracts ‚Üí Yield Generation
                                                    ‚Üì
                                            Returns to User
```

1. **You deposit** Bitcoin-pegged stablecoins (MUSD) into smart contracts
2. **Smart contracts** manage your funds transparently and securely
3. **Yield protocols** generate returns through DeFi strategies
4. **You earn** yields automatically, visible in real-time

<Callout type="info">
**Your funds, your control**: Unlike traditional banks, KhipuVault smart contracts are non-custodial. You can withdraw anytime (except during ROSCA commitments).
</Callout>

### 3. üîÑ Rotating Pool (ROSCA/Pasanaku)

**Traditional rotating savings digitized.**

Traditional communities use "tandas" (Mexico), "pasanakus" (Bolivia), or "ROSCAs" (Rotating Savings and Credit Associations). KhipuVault brings this proven model to blockchain.

**How it works:**
- Group of people commit to monthly deposits
- Each month, one member receives the full pot
- Rotation continues until everyone has received
- No interest, no banks - just community trust

**Use case**: *"10 friends each deposit $100/month. Each month, one receives $1,000."*

[Learn more ‚Üí](/docs/products/rotating-pool)

- Withdraw your principal anytime

**Use case**: *"I want the excitement of lottery but without losing my money."*

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my Bitcoin safe?
Yes. Your funds are in non-custodial smart contracts audited by security firms. You hold your private keys.

### Can I withdraw anytime?
Yes for Individual Savings, Community Pools, and Prize Pools. ROSCA pools require commitment until payout.

### What are the yields?
Yields vary by product and market conditions. Current APY is displayed on each pool.

### Do I need Bitcoin?
You need MUSD (Mezo USD), a Bitcoin-pegged stablecoin on Mezo. Use the faucet for testnet.

### Is this available in my country?
KhipuVault is a decentralized protocol accessible worldwide. Check your local regulations.
