## Understanding Prize Pools

### What is a No-Loss Lottery?

A revolutionary savings model that combines:

**Traditional Lottery**: Chance to win big prizes
**Savings Account**: Keep your deposit forever
**Yield Generation**: Your deposit earns interest

**Key Innovation**: Prize pool uses the YIELD to fund prizes, not your principal!

```mermaid
graph LR
    A[Your Deposit] --> B[Earns Yield]
    B --> C[Yield Goes to Prize Pool]
    C --> D[Winners Selected]
    D --> E[You Keep Principal]
    E --> F[Enter Next Draw]
```

### How Prize Pools Work

1. **Deposit**: You deposit Bitcoin (e.g., 0.1 BTC)
2. **Earn Tickets**: Each deposit unit = tickets (0.01 BTC = 1 ticket)
3. **Yield Accrues**: Your deposit earns yield (~8-10% APY)
4. **Prize Pool**: All participants' yield combined into prize pool
5. **Drawing**: Random selection of winners
6. **Keep Deposit**: Win or lose, you keep your 0.1 BTC
7. **Repeat**: Automatically entered in next draw

<Callout type="success">
  **No-Loss Guarantee**: You NEVER lose your principal. Worst case: you earn 0% APY. Best case: win massive prizes!
</Callout>

### Prize Pool Types

#### Daily Prize Pool
- Drawings every 24 hours
- Smaller prizes (0.01 - 0.1 BTC)
- More frequent chances to win
- Best for: Regular engagement

#### Weekly Prize Pool
- Drawings every 7 days
- Medium prizes (0.1 - 0.5 BTC)
- Better odds than daily
- Best for: Balanced approach

#### Monthly Prize Pool
- Drawings every 30 days
- Large prizes (0.5 - 5 BTC)
- Accumulates more yield
- Best for: Big prize hunting

#### Mega Jackpot
- Drawings quarterly
- Massive prizes (5+ BTC)
- Lower odds but life-changing
- Best for: Long-term players

## Step 2: Evaluate Prize Pools

### Key Metrics to Review

#### Expected Value (EV)

Calculate if a pool is worth entering:

```
Your Deposit:        0.1 BTC (10 tickets)
Total Tickets:       10,000 tickets
Prize Pool:          1 BTC
Your Win Chance:     10/10,000 = 0.1%
Expected Win:        1 BTC Ã— 0.1% = 0.001 BTC
Alternative APY:     ~8% = 0.008 BTC/year yield

EV Comparison:       Prize pool slightly lower EV
But: Excitement value + chance at lump sum!
```

#### Pool Health Indicators

Look for:
- **Active Participants**: More players = bigger prizes
- **Consistent Growth**: Pool size increasing = healthy
- **Regular Drawings**: On-time draws = trustworthy
- **Winner Verification**: Provable fairness + on-chain verification
- **Audit Status**: Smart contract audited

#### Prize Distribution

Evaluate prize structure:

**Top-Heavy** (90% to 1st place):
- Massive grand prize
- Few winners overall
- High risk/reward

**Distributed** (many smaller prizes):
- More winners
- Better average return
- Feels more rewarding

**Balanced** (mix):
- Big prize + many consolation prizes
- Best of both worlds

<Callout type="warning">
  **Realistic Expectations**: Prize pools are FUN and EXCITING, but individual savings pools offer better guaranteed returns. Use prize pools for a small portion of your portfolio.
</Callout>

## Common Questions

### What if I need to withdraw before drawing?

**No problem!**
- Withdraw anytime
- Get full principal back
- Forfeit tickets for that drawing
- Can re-enter any time

### Do more tickets always mean better odds?

**Yes, proportionally**:
- 1 ticket = 0.01% chance
- 10 tickets = 0.1% chance (10x better)
- 100 tickets = 1% chance (100x better)

But total pool size matters too!

### Can I buy tickets without depositing?

**No**:
- Tickets = representation of your deposit
- 0.01 BTC deposit = 1 ticket
- Ensures you have skin in the game
- Prevents infinite ticket buying

### What happens to unclaimed prizes?

After 30-day deadline:
- Returns to prize pool
- Increases next drawing's prize
- Benefits all participants

### Are there "better" times to enter?

**Early in drawing period**:
- Fewer participants (better odds)
- More time to accumulate tickets
- Lower prize initially

**Late in drawing period**:
- See current prize size
- More participants (worse odds)
- Prize fully grown

**Verdict**: Enter early, stay consistent

## Troubleshooting

### Entry Transaction Failed

**Solutions**:
1. Check sufficient BTC + gas fees
2. Verify pool is accepting entries
3. Check drawing hasn't closed for this round
4. Try smaller entry amount
5. Contact support if persists

### Tickets Not Showing

**Reasons**:
1. Transaction still confirming (wait 1-2 min)
2. UI not refreshed (hard refresh page)
3. Wrong wallet connected
4. Entry failed (check transaction status)

### Prize Claim Failing

**Common Issues**:
1. Already claimed
2. Claim period expired
3. Not actually a winner (double-check)
4. Smart contract issue

**Solution**: Contact support with transaction hash

### Lower Odds Than Expected

**Reality Check**:
- Your tickets / total tickets = your odds
- Pool growing = your odds decreasing
- Still better than traditional lottery!
- Remember: You can't lose principal
