# KhipuVault Mainnet Launch Campaign Plan

## Community-First DeFi Savings Platform on Mezo

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** February 2026
**Team:** 3 core members (Developer, Brand Designer, Social Media Manager)
**Timeline:** Q1-Q4 2026

---

## Executive Summary

KhipuVault is a decentralized Bitcoin savings platform built on Mezo blockchain, winner of 1st place in the "Financial Access and Mass Adoption" track at the Mezo Hackathon. This campaign plan outlines a realistic, community-first approach to mainnet launch with limited resources, focusing on organic growth, user education, and sustainable adoption.

**Core Strategy:** Start small, validate with real users, scale gradually based on feedback and traction.

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch Foundation (Q1 2026)

**Goal:** Build minimum viable community (MVC) and validate product-market fit

### Week 1-4: Infrastructure Setup

**Developer Tasks:**

- ✅ Complete security audit requirements
- ✅ Testnet optimization and bug fixes
- ✅ Smart contract documentation
- ✅ Developer guides for integration
- ✅ Prepare mainnet deployment scripts

**Brand Designer Tasks:**

- ✅ Finalize brand guidelines document
- ✅ Create launch asset library (logos, banners, templates)
- ✅ Design educational infographics (how savings pools work)
- ✅ Prepare video explainer storyboard
- ✅ Social media visual templates

**Social Media Manager Tasks:**

- ✅ Set up community platforms (Discord, Telegram)
- ✅ Create content calendar for 3 months
- ✅ Draft launch announcement copy
- ✅ Research and list potential micro-influencers
- ✅ Create pinned messages & FAQ documents

### Week 5-8: Community Seeding

**Content Strategy (Weekly Output):**

- 5 Twitter/X posts (educational threads about Bitcoin DeFi)
- 2 Medium articles (technical deep-dives)
- 1 video explainer (2-3 minutes)
- Daily Discord/Telegram engagement (15-30 min)

**Channels & Goals:**

- **Discord:** 50-100 early adopters (quality over quantity)
- **Twitter/X:** 200-500 followers (engaged, not bots)
- **Telegram:** 30-50 active members
- **Medium:** 2,000-5,000 views per article

**Collaboration Strategy:**

- Identify 3-5 complementary Mezo projects for cross-promotion
- Reach out to Mezo community ambassadors
- Join relevant DeFi and Bitcoin Twitter Spaces as listeners
- Comment authentically on related project posts

### Week 9-12: Beta Testing Campaign

**Goal:** 20-30 real users testing on testnet

**Execution:**

- Announce closed beta via Twitter/X and Discord
- Quest-based whitelist (not random):
  - Follow KhipuVault on X and join Discord
  - Share one use case for Bitcoin savings in DeFi
  - Complete onboarding tutorial on testnet
  - Provide structured feedback

**Developer:** Monitor usage, fix critical bugs, document issues
**Designer:** Create user journey visuals, testimonial graphics
**Social Media Manager:** Daily updates, user highlights, feedback collection

## Phase 2: Mainnet Launch (Q2 2026)

**Goal:** Successful mainnet deployment with 100-200 early adopters

### Pre-Launch Week (Week 13)

**Monday-Tuesday: Final Preparations**

- Smart contract deployment to mainnet (Developer)

- All marketing assets finalized (Designer)
- Launch announcement scheduled (Social Media Manager)

**Wednesday: Soft Launch**

- Deploy to mainnet (non-public)

**Thursday-Friday: Pre-Announcement Hype**

- Teaser posts on Twitter/X (countdown)
- Behind-the-scenes content (team working, code snippets)
- Partner announcements (if any secured)
- AMA announcement for launch day

### Launch Week (Week 14)

- Twitter thread announcing mainnet launch
- Medium article: "KhipuVault is Live on Mezo Mainnet"

- **Friday:** Community spotlight or user story

- **Content Collaborations:** Guest posts with complementary projects
- **Twitter Spaces:** Co-host with other Mezo builders (bi-weekly)
- **Case Studies:** Document real user savings journeys

**Metrics to Track:**

- Mainnet TVL (Total Value Locked)

## Community Building Strategy

### Platform Strategy

**Discord (Primary Hub)**

- **Channels:**
  - #welcome (onboarding)
  - #announcements (team only)
  - #general-chat (community)
  - #support (user help)
  - #feedback (product ideas)
  - #dev-corner (technical discussions)
  - #content-creators (for ambassadors)

- **Moderation:** Social Media Manager + 2-3 community moderators (volunteer)
- **Events:** Weekly voice chats, monthly AMAs
- **Bots:** Basic moderation + welcome bot

**Twitter/X (Discovery & Virality)**

- **Focus:** Educational threads, product updates, community highlights
- **Engagement:** Reply to mentions within 2 hours during work hours
- **Strategy:** 70% educational, 20% product updates, 10% community/fun
- **Hashtags:** #Bitcoin #DeFi #Mezo #KhipuVault

**Telegram (Real-Time Support)**

- **Focus:** Quick questions, community chat, announcements
- **Admin:** Social Media Manager
- **Guidelines:** No price talk, no spam, helpful attitude
- **Integration:** Bridge important Discord announcements

**Medium (Long-Form Content)**

- **Frequency:** 2-4 articles per month
- **Topics:** Technical deep-dives, user stories, market insights, tutorials
- **Distribution:** Cross-post to dev.to, HackerNoon

### Ambassador Program (Launch Q3)

**Criteria:**

- Active community member for 2+ months
- Created at least one savings pool on mainnet
- Demonstrated helpful attitude in Discord/Telegram
- Created user-generated content (optional but preferred)

**Responsibilities:**

- Answer basic questions in community channels
- Create user-generated content (tutorials, tweets, videos)
- Provide feedback on new features
- Represent KhipuVault in their own networks

**Benefits:**

- Early access to new features
- Direct line to team
- Recognition and spotlight
- Exclusive ambassador role in Discord

## Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** Critical smart contract bug discovered post-launch

- **Mitigation:** Comprehensive audit before mainnet, bug bounty program
- **Contingency:** Emergency pause mechanism, transparent communication, user fund protection plan

**Risk:** Low mainnet activity / TVL

- **Mitigation:** Beta testing with real users, feedback iteration
- **Contingency:** Pivot messaging, identify and fix UX barriers, increase educational content

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Broader crypto market downturn

- **Mitigation:** Focus on product utility, not speculative appeal
- **Contingency:** Double down on education, emphasize savings philosophy, build in bear market

**Risk:** Competitor launches similar product

- **Mitigation:** Strong community moat, unique Mezo-native features
- **Contingency:** Emphasize differentiation, accelerate feature development, deepen partnerships

### Community Risks

**Risk:** Toxic community members or spam

- **Mitigation:** Clear community guidelines, active moderation
- **Contingency:** Warning system, bans if necessary, transparency about decisions

**Risk:** Low engagement despite growth efforts

- **Mitigation:** A/B test content, ask for feedback directly, analyze metrics
- **Contingency:** Pivot content strategy, try new platforms, community co-creation

## Conclusion

This mainnet launch campaign is designed to be **realistic, flexible, and sustainable** for a 3-person team. Success will not come from massive marketing budgets or viral hype, but from:

1. **Building trust** through security, transparency, and consistent communication
2. **Solving real problems** for users who want to save in Bitcoin
3. **Growing organically** through word-of-mouth and authentic community
4. **Iterating quickly** based on user feedback
5. **Playing the long game** - building a product that lasts beyond bull market hype

The campaign prioritizes **quality over quantity**, **education over hype**, and **community over vanity metrics**. With the Mezo ecosystem grant supporting development, the team can focus on execution without distraction.

**Next Steps:**

1. Team review and alignment on this plan
2. Assign ownership of each phase
3. Set up tracking systems for all KPIs
4. Begin Phase 1 execution immediately
